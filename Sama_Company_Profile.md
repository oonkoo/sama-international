Company Overview

SAMA Service Company Ltd. has been a
trusted name in the Saudi construction and facility management sector since
2019. With a strong foundation built on integrity, technical excellence, and
client-focused solutions, we deliver comprehensive services that meet the
diverse needs of our clients. Established in 2019 in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. SAMA
SERVICE COMPANY LTD. is committed to high-quality construction, MEP, and
facility management services. We combine expertise, innovation, and dedication
to provide complete solutions for complex projects.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To provide high-quality

Mission: To be recognized as a leading
construction and facility management company in Saudi Arabia, renowned for

**Organizational Structure & Team **

SAMA Service Company Ltd. maintains a clear
and efficient organizational structure that ensures smooth communication,
effective project control, and accountability at every level.

Our management framework is built to
support multiple projects simultaneously while maintaining quality and safety
standards.

Leadership and Management: The company is
led by a Managing Director who provides strategic guidance and oversees all
business operations. Reporting to the Managing Director are the Operations
Manager, Finance Manager, and Department Heads for Construction, MEP, and
Facility Management.

Technical and Project Teams: Each project
is managed by a dedicated Project Manager supported by qualified engineers,
site supervisors, and skilled technicians. Our teams are equipped with the
technical knowledge and practical experience necessary to deliver complex
projects within schedule and budget.

Support Departments: Our success is
reinforced by specialized departments for Procurement, HSE (Health, Safety, and
Environment), Human Resources, and Administration — each playing a vital role
in ensuring efficient operations and compliance with industry standards.

Team Competence: SAMA Service Company
values professional development and continuous improvement. Our engineers and
managers hold relevant local and international certifications, and we regularly
conduct training to maintain the highest standards of quality, safety, and
performance.
